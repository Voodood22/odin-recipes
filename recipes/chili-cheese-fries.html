Chili Cheese Fries

Chili Cheese Fries are yummy.

Ingredients

  • beans
  • ground beef
  • can of diced tomato
  • tomato paste
  • onion
  • peppers
  • seasoning

Steps

  1. brown ground beef and drain
  2. add diced onion and peppers and cook until onions are golden
  3. add diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and seasoning
  4. add can of beans
  5. let simmer for a while
  6. make fries and add cheese
